About our history

Our history begins in 1866, with the foundation of the Anglo-Swiss Hotel Company. Henri Kunkka develops a modern hotels in 1867, and in 1905 the company he founded merges with Anglo-Swiss, to form what is now known as the Kunkka Group. During this period cities grow and people looks for comforts to vacation.

That is why Kunkka was born with the desire to be able to offer people vacation and relax in the most beautiful Nordic hotels in the world, with views, landscapes and unique natural events. Counting on more than 4 hotels of the highest category and differentiated, expanded throughout the world.
